# General Questions

## How do I join the server?
Connect to **mc.jarton.me** in your Minecraft client. We support both Java and Bedrock editions.

## What version of Minecraft do you support?
We support the latest version of Minecraft Java Edition. Bedrock players can also join through our cross-platform support.

## Is the server free to play?
Yes! JartonMC is completely free to play. We offer optional premium features and cosmetics to support the server.

---

# Gameplay Questions

## How do I claim land?
Use `/claim` to protect your builds. New players get free claim blocks, and you can earn more through playtime and voting.

## Can I play with friends?
Absolutely! Invite friends, create parties with `/party`, and work together on builds and quests.

## Are there any rules I should know?
Yes, please read our [server rules](/rules) to ensure a great experience for everyone.

## How do I earn money?
You can earn gold through voting, completing quests, jobs, trading, and various gameplay activities.

---

# Technical Questions  

## I'm having connection issues. What should I do?
1. Check if the server is online at our Discord
2. Verify you're using the correct address: **mc.jarton.me**
3. Try restarting your Minecraft client
4. Contact staff if the issue persists

## My items disappeared! Can you help?
Contact staff immediately with details about when and where it happened. We keep backups and can often restore lost items.

## How do I report bugs or cheaters?
Use `/ticket` in-game or report issues in our Discord server. Staff will investigate promptly.
