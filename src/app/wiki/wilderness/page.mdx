# The Wilderness

Beyond the safety of spawn lies the vast wilderness - a dangerous but rewarding area filled with custom biomes, structures, and challenges.

---

# Custom Biomes

JartonMC features several unique biomes not found in vanilla Minecraft:

## Enchanted Forest
- Dense magical trees with glowing leaves
- Home to rare magical creatures
- Contains valuable enchanted resources

## Crystal Caverns  
- Underground biome filled with crystal formations
- Source of rare gems and minerals
- Dangerous crystal golems guard the treasures

## Volcanic Wasteland
- Harsh terrain with lava flows and ash storms
- Fire-resistant mobs and unique ores
- Extreme temperatures require special gear

# Structures & Dungeons

<img src="/wiki/generic_dungeon.webp" className="large_img" />

The wilderness contains numerous structures to discover:

- **Ancient Temples**: Puzzle-filled structures with valuable loot
- **Abandoned Mines**: Deep underground complexes with rare ores
- **Wizard Towers**: Magical structures containing spell components
- **Bandit Camps**: PvP-enabled areas with high-risk, high-reward gameplay

<div className="warning">
Many wilderness areas have increased difficulty and PvP may be enabled. Always come prepared!

# Survival Tips

Essential tips for wilderness exploration:

1. **Bring supplies**: Food, tools, and emergency gear
2. **Travel in groups**: Safety in numbers
3. **Mark your path**: Use coordinates or landmarks  
4. **Check PvP status**: Some areas allow player combat
5. **Respect claims**: Don't grief other players' builds

---

# Transportation

Getting around the wilderness efficiently:

- **Horses**: Tame and breed horses for faster travel
- **Boats**: Navigate rivers and coastal areas
- **Elytra**: Late-game flying for experienced players
- **Waypoints**: Set custom teleport points (premium feature)
