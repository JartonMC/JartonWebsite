# Economy System

JartonMC features a comprehensive economy system with multiple currencies and trading mechanics.

---

# Gold Currency

Gold is the primary currency on JartonMC. You can earn gold through various activities:

- **Voting**: Earn 500 gold per vote site
- **Quests**: Complete daily and weekly quests for gold rewards  
- **Trading**: Buy and sell items with other players
- **Mining**: Find gold nuggets and ingots while mining
- **Jobs**: Work various server jobs to earn steady income

---

# Nectar System

Nectar is a special secondary currency used for premium items and services.

<div className="info">
Nectar can be obtained through special events, achievements, and premium activities.
</div>

---

# Player Trading

Set up shops and trade with other players using our economy system:

- Create chest shops to sell your items
- Use `/shop` to browse the server marketplace
- Trade directly with players using `/trade`
- Check current market prices with `/prices`

---

# Economy Commands

Essential commands for managing your finances:

- `/balance` - Check your current gold and nectar
- `/pay <player> <amount>` - Send gold to another player
- `/shop` - Open the server shop interface
- `/ah` - Access the auction house
- `/jobs` - View available jobs and your progress
