# Server Rules & Guidelines

Welcome to JartonMC! These rules help maintain a fun, fair, and friendly environment for all players.

<div className="warning">
Breaking these rules may result in warnings, temporary mutes/bans, or permanent removal from the server depending on severity.
</div>

---

# Basic Conduct

## Be Respectful
- Treat all players and staff with respect
- No harassment, bullying, or hate speech
- Keep discussions civil and constructive

## Family-Friendly Environment  
- Keep chat and builds appropriate for all ages
- No NSFW content, excessive profanity, or mature themes
- Use common sense - if you wouldn't show it to a child, don't post it here

## No Impersonation
- Don't pretend to be staff members or other players
- Don't use names or skins that could confuse other players
- Respect others' identities and privacy

---

# Gameplay Rules

## Fair Play Only
- No hacking, cheating, or exploiting bugs
- Prohibited: X-ray, fly hacks, speed hacks, auto-clickers, etc.
- Report bugs instead of abusing them

## Respect Others' Property
- No griefing, stealing, or destroying others' builds
- Respect land claims and private areas
- Ask permission before building near others

## PvP Guidelines
- PvP is only allowed in designated areas
- No spawn killing or unfair advantages
- Respect combat logging rules

---

# Building & Claims

## Claim Your Land
- Use `/claim` to protect your builds
- Don't claim excessive amounts of unused land
- Keep claims reasonable and purposeful

## Build Appropriately  
- No inappropriate structures, signs, or symbols
- Clean up temporary builds and don't leave "floating trees"
- Build at least 100 blocks from others unless given permission

## Economy Rules
- No item duplication or economy exploitation
- Trade fairly - no scamming other players
- Don't abuse shop systems or auction house

---

# Communication

## Chat Guidelines
- English only in global chat (other languages in private messages)
- No excessive spam, caps, or repeated messages
- No advertising other servers or external links

## Getting Help
- Use `/ticket` for support issues
- Don't ask for free items, ranks, or special treatment
- Be patient - staff will help when available

---

# Consequences

Violations may result in:
- **Minor issues**: Verbal warnings or temporary mutes
- **Moderate issues**: Temporary bans (1-7 days)
- **Serious issues**: Permanent bans

<div className="info">
We believe in second chances! Most bans can be appealed through our Discord server. Staff will review each case fairly.
</div>

---

# Questions?

If you're unsure about any rule or need clarification:
- Ask in Discord: **discord.gg/jartonmc**  
- Create a ticket: `/ticket`
- Contact staff directly

Remember: These rules exist to keep JartonMC fun for everyone. When in doubt, use common sense and treat others how you'd like to be treated!
